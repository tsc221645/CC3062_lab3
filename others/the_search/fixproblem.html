You struggle with the snowmobile, meanwhile, you are feeling the deadly cold.

Suddenly, you see a shadow in the distance, moving really fast. You get scared and decide to pull out your gun.

The shadow gets closer to you and you get ready to fire. When Suddenly your gun gets kicked off your hands and then you see it...

A penguin? In the middle of nowhere. You notice something weird about it so you try to catch him but in the matter of milliseconds, it flies away from you.

You have just witnessed the flying penguin. Looks like you accomplished your mission, in fact the flying penguin exists...
