Walking to the airport

You walked over an hour to get to the airport in freezing conditions but you finally reached it.

Someone from the airport crew notices you and helps you get inside to get warmed up and changed into adequate clothes.

You talk to this person, his name is Joseph. You tell him about what happened to the plane and he assures you they are already taking care of it.

You have to continue with your adventure. They told you this flying penguin is found 50km away from in the airport, in an iceberg.

You decide to...
