The back of the plane

You grab your stuff and make it to the back of the plane. You hope for the best.

The plane crashes and you fall unconscious for a while. When you wake up all you feel is cold around you and an eerie silence.

Then you realize, you are fully surrounded by snow. Nobody else survived.

You start to get yourself out of the snow, but you only manage to get half of your body out.

And there it ends.... your adventure comes to an end.
